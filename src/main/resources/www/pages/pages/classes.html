Beginner Courses

In our Ballroom Dance Classes for Beginners, you will learn the basic steps and techniques of both Standard Ballroom and Latin Ballroom dance styles. The classes are great fun and taught in a friendly and supportive atmosphere by some of the UK's finest ballroom teachers. This course is perfect for those with little or no ballroom dance experience, interested in learning the basics of a variety of styles and gaining the ability to socially dance each of them. Ballroom Dance Classes for Beginners - Course Content

Within our Beginners Ballroom Dance Programme you will:

  • Learn basics of a selection of Standard Ballroom Dances
  • Learn basics of a selection of Latin Ballroom Dances
  • Learn the techniques of leading and following
  • Have the opportunity to dance with a variety of partners in a social atmosphere
  • From nights out across London to weddings and other social events, you will be able to utilise your newly acquired dancing skills.

Upon completion of our Beginner Ballroom Dance Programme, you will have learnt basic steps and techniques necessary to socially dance six different ballroom dance styles.
No partner is required to join the course.

Intermediate courses

In our Ballroom Dance Intermediate Courses, you will build upon the techniques learnt in our Improvers Programme and begin learning routines in both Standard Ballroom and Latin Ballroom dance styles. This course will follow Bronze Level syllabus.
This ballroom lesson is perfect for those who have completed an Improvers course or equivalent. Please get in touch if you are not sure about your level. Ballroom Dance - Intermediate Course Content
Within the Intermediate Ballroom Dance programme you will:

  • Learn Standard Ballroom syllabus routines (Foxtrot, Waltz, Tango, Quickstep)
  • Learn Latin Ballroom syllabus routines (Cha Cha, Rumba, Jive, Samba)
  • Focus on connection with partner and improved dance positions
  • Have the opportunity to dance with a variety of partners in a social atmosphere

This class will consist of 1 hour of instruction and a 15 minute social (either before or after instruction), where you will have the opportunity to practise the steps you have learnt in a social environment.
No partner is required to join the course.

Advanced courses

Our Latin Ballroom Dance masterclass is a fun balanced workshop where you'll spend half the session learning Latin Ballroom technique before putting this into practice in a playful and energetic choreographed Latin routine.
This class is perfect for ballroom dancers of all levels who wish to refine their Latin dance skills in a fun-focused technical workshop. You will refresh Latin steps, improve and experiment with connection types, before putting the steps to music in the choreography.
The sessions are highly social. Whether you sign-up as an individual, with a partner, or with a group of friends, it's a great opportunity to meet new people whilst learning something new. Workshop content During the workshop you will:

  • Cover and refine Latin steps
  • Work on Cuban Action: using your hips in Latin dancing
  • Push & Pull: experiment with connection types
  • Learn playful Ballroom choreography to dynamic and exciting music
  • Add flair and expression to your dancing
  • Work on your Latin and Ballroom technique

As with all of our ballroom dancing lesssons, you will receive friendly feedback from your tutor throughout the workshop.
